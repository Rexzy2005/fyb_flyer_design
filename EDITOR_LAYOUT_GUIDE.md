## Interactive Elements

### Buttons
- **Back**: Outline style, disabled when on first section
- **Next**: Solid gradient, shows when not on final section
- **Download**: Full gradient (green), shows on final section only

### Form Inputs
- **Text**: Clean borders, focus states
- **Image**: Drag-drop zone with emoji
- **Date**: Native HTML5 with styling

### Progress Indicators
- **Bar at top**: Overall progress percentage
- **In form**: Required field count (5/8)
- **Visual feedback**: Badges and icons

## States & Transitions

### Button States
- **Enabled**: Full opacity, cursor pointer
- **Hover**: Color intensifies, slight scale
- **Disabled**: Reduced opacity, cursor not-allowed
- **Active**: Slight shadow, pressed feel

### Form Field States
- **Empty**: Gray border, placeholder text
- **Focused**: Purple border, shadow
- **Filled**: Shows value, no border emphasis
- **Image uploaded**: Badge with checkmark

### Modals
- **Payment**: Paystack checkout overlay
- **Design ready**: Success modal with preview
- **Loading**: Spinner overlay
